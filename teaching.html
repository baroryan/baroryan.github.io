Teaching

Throughout my career, I’ve had the privilege of teaching and mentoring students in geodynamics and several undergraduate courses. Building on these experiences, one of my proudest achievements was designing, leading, and securing funding for a twelve-day field trip to Peru. This trip provided twelve graduate students from LDEO with a unique opportunity to explore first-hand key geoscience phenomena, including mountain building, earthquakes, tropical glaciers, and the impacts of El Niño.
